MASTER OF COMPUTER APPLICATION

A Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a two-year postgraduate degree program focused on the theory and application of computer science and software development. It provides advanced knowledge in areas like programming, database management, networking, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity, preparing graduates for roles in the IT industry. Eligibility typically requires a bachelor's degree in a related field with mathematics as a subject and a minimum percentage of marks, with some programs requiring entrance exam
